About Us

Hariom Bhadana

Founder & Lead Instructor

With over a decade of experience in video production and post-production, Hariom founded Esech Materials to share professional-grade resources and knowledge with aspiring editors worldwide. His work has been featured in major film festivals and commercial projects for global brands.

Our Mission

At Esech Materials, we believe that high-quality educational resources should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to democratize professional video editing knowledge by providing affordable courses and free materials that help creators at all levels improve their skills.

We focus on practical, real-world techniques that you can apply immediately to your projects, whether you're editing for YouTube, clients, or personal creative expression.
