Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my purchased courses?

After purchase, you'll receive an email with a Google Drive link to download all course materials. The link provides lifetime access to the content, including any future updates.

Are the free materials really free?

Yes! All materials in our Free Materials section are completely free to download and use in your personal or commercial projects. No hidden fees or subscriptions required.

What software do I need for the courses?

Our courses cover various editing software including Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve, and Final Cut Pro. Each course listing specifies the required software.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day refund policy if you're not satisfied with your purchase. Please contact our support team with your order details to initiate a refund.

Can I request specific materials or courses?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new content. Use our contact form to let us know what you'd like to see, and we'll consider it for future updates.
